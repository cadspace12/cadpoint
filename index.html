About Our Institute

Transforming Passion into Profession Since 2010

CAD POINT is a premier jewellery designing institute dedicated to nurturing creative talent and providing industry-relevant education in jewellery design. Our mission is to bridge the gap between traditional jewellery craftsmanship and modern digital design techniques.

With our team of highly experienced trainers and state-of-the-art facilities, we offer comprehensive training programs that cover all aspects of jewellery design, from manual sketching to advanced CAD techniques.

Our unique approach combines theoretical knowledge with practical hands-on training, ensuring our students are job-ready upon completion of their courses.
